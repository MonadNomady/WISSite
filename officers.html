Fall 2024 Officers

President
Neuroscience MajorMegan Brown



Megan Brown is the current President for WIS and a Neuroscience major, with a minor in Psychology. She works as a Research Assistant in the TARDIS Lab, is a McNair Scholar, and an Air Force veteran. She also serves as Speaker of the Council for the College of Sciences Student Council. Megan has three children, and a husband who is in the Air Force. She plans to go onto graduate school and pursue a career in research, with a focus on Autism Spectrum Disorder. For fun, she likes to read, cook/bake, and run. A fun fact is that she has an irrational fear of the Kool-Aid Man.
