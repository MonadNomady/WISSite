WOMEN IN STEM AT UTSA

ABOUT US

Welcome to Women In STEM at UTSA! We are a vibrant community dedicated to empowering and supporting women pursuing majors in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM). Our mission is to foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among women in STEM fields, providing them with the resources, mentorship, and opportunities they need to thrive academically and professionally. At Women In STEM, we believe in the power of community to inspire and uplift. Whether you're a freshman exploring your interests or a seasoned STEM veteran, you'll find a welcoming environment here. Through networking events, workshops, guest speakers, and mentorship programs, we strive to create meaningful connections and empower each other to reach our fullest potential. Join us as we work together to break down barriers, shatter stereotypes, and build a brighter future for women in STEM. Together, we can achieve anything!
